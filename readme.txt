Turn any photo into loads of fun.  Put photo puzzles on any post or page.  Even make standalone photo post to share with your audience.

== Description ==

###Quick Start Guide

* [photo puzzle quick start guide at thinklandingpages.com] (http://www.thinklandingpages.com/photo-puzzle/)

An easy way to place a photo puzzle with content on your Wordpress site.

###What you get when you use the Photo Puzzle photo puzzle

*  Place a photo puzzle with a simple shortcode [photo-puzzle url="urltophoto"]
*  Put photo puzzle on any post or page.
*  Create full photo puzzle post with description from admin panel
*  Make an unlimited number of photo puzzles


###Add photo puzzle to any post or page

Whether you are writing a post about your company or another company, you can include a photo puzzle.


###Photo Puzzle 

Add as photo puzzle to your site as you like.  The Photo Puzzle lets you create photo puzzle and save them for when ever you are ready to use them.



== Installation ==


1. Upload `photo puzzle` to the `/wp-content/photo puzzles/` directory
1. Activate the photo puzzle through the 'Photo Puzzles' menu in WordPress
1. Click **Photo Puzzle** on the admin menu to enable and set your options.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to know how to program or design? =

No.  The photo puzzle does the programming and design.

= Is there a limit to the number of photo puzzle I can create? =
No, unlimited photo puzzle can be created.

= Can the photo puzzle be put on my homepage? =
Yes, you can put photo puzzle on your homepage by using the Photo Puzzle photo puzzle shortcode on you homepage.

= Can I put photo puzzle on any post or page? =
Yes, use the photo puzzle shortcode, [photo-puzzle url="urltophoto"] on your pages and posts.
